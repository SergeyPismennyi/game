Space Warrior

About

Space Warrior is a top-down game where it is necessary to destroy infinitely appearing enemy ships. The purpose of this game is collect as many points as possible. For this you must destroy as many enemies as possible.

Screenshots

Controls

Movement

Use 'WASD' or arrows to move the ship on the map

Aiming

Use the mouse to point at enemy ships

Shooting

Use the left mouse button to fire at enemies
